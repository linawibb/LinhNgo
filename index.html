About me

I am a highly motivated and enthusiastic first year graphics design student who takes pride in producing work of a high calibre. As an international student from Vietnam, I believe I can bring a unique design perspective and stylistic influence to my work. I am also keen to learn from Australian designers to further my design career in Australia.
